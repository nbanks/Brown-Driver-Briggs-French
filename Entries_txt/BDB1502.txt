=== BDB1502 H1436 ===
Biblical Hebrew

גְּדַלְיָה
proper name, masculine (Yah is great) —

@@SPLIT:sense@@

1. governor of Judea appointed by Nebuchadnezzar Jer 40:5; Jer 40:8; Jer 41:16.

@@SPLIT:sense@@

2. son of Amariah, a son of Hezekiah Zeph 1:1.

@@SPLIT:sense@@

3. priest of the sons of Jeshua Ezra 10:18.
