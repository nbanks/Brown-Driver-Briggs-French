=== BDB208 H425 ===
Biblical Hebrew

II. אֵלָה
proper name, masculine (terebinth, compare Gen 49:21 below I. אֵלָה)

## SPLIT 1 sense

1. a chief of Edom Gen 36:41 = 1Chr 1:52 (= אֵילַת
proper name [of a location] ? see Di).

## SPLIT 2 sense

2. son of Baasha; reigned two years in Israel 1Kgs 16:6; 1Kgs 16:8; 1Kgs 16:13; 1Kgs 16:14.

## SPLIT 3 sense

3. father of Hoshea who was last king of Israel 2Kgs 15:30; 2Kgs 17:1; 2Kgs 18:1; 2Kgs 18:9.

## SPLIT 4 sense

4. a son of Caleb 1Chr 4:15 (twice in verse).

## SPLIT 5 sense

5. a son of Uzzi 1Chr 9:8.
