Biblical Hebrew

II. כְּבָר
proper name [of a river]
Kebar, a river (or perhaps a canal) of Babylonia, not at present identified, by which the exiles,
among whom Ezekiel ministered, were settled; always in the phrase נְהַר כְּבָר
Ezek 1:1; Ezek 1:3; Ezek 3:15; Ezek 3:23; Ezek 10:15;
Ezek 10:20;
Ezek 10:22;
Ezek 43:3
(compare Sm^
Ezek 1:1
^ Del^Par. 47 f., 184^).
