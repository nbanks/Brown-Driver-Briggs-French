Biblical Hebrew

אֶלְקוֺשִׁי
adjective [of a people] with article Nahum the Elkoshite Nah 1:1 (perhaps = from Elkosh, but
locality unknown; identified by Jer^Comm.^ with a village
Elcesi in Galilee; compare also Capernaum (= כְּפַר נַחוּם ?); others
(improbably) with an Elkosh on east bank of Tigris, near Mosul).
On etymology, and site of place, see Da^Nahum, etc., 9 ff.^ GASm^Twelve Proph. ii.
79f^.
