=== BDB3010 H2657 ===
Biblical Hebrew

חֶפְצִיבָֿהּ
proper name, feminine (my delight is in her; compare Phoenician חפצבעל
) —

@@SPLIT:sense@@

1. mother of king Manasseh 2Kgs 21:1.

@@SPLIT:sense@@

2.
figurative name of Zion Isa 62:4, here explained by כִּי חפץ יהוה בָּךְ
for Yahweh delighteth in thee.
