Biblical Hebrew

אתי, i.e. אַתִּי, the older & more original form of אַתְּ
thou (feminine), preserved, probably dialectically, 7 t. in
Kt
, Judg 17:2
; 1Kgs 14:2
; 2Kgs 4:16; 2Kgs 4:23; 2Kgs 8:1; Jer 4:30; Ezek 36:13.
(V. above as in Syriac, the י may not have been fully sounded: the Masoretes direct אַתְּ
to be everywhere read.)
