הֵימָן
proper name, masculine
Heman (faithful, compare Aramaic מְהֵימָן,
[placeholder282: Placeholders/282.gif]) a wise man with whom Solomon is compared 1Kgs 5:11, where app. son of Mahol (Klo

3+1 other, & all called sons of Zerah of Judah; Heman appears Ps 88:1 also as the Ezrahite (see below זרח),
compare Ethan above; in other passages Heman is a Levite; specifically Kohathite, son of Joel, called the
singer (הַמְשׁוֺרֵר) 1Chr 6:18 (|| Asaph v.1Chr 6:24, Ethan v.1Chr 6:29); Heman, Asaph & Ethan named as the
singers (הַמְשֹׁרְרִים) 1Chr 15:17; 1Chr 15:19 compare Heman & Jeduthun 1Chr 16:41; 1Chr 16:42 (|| Asaph 1Chr 16:37);
