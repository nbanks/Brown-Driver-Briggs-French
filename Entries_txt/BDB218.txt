Biblical Hebrew

I. [אוּלַי] proper name [of a river]
Ulai, Eulaeus (Assyrian
Ulâi
, compare Dl^Pa 329^ Greek Εὐλαιος)
only אוּלָ֑י, river of Elam Dan 8:2; Dan 8:16 = (at least in lower part) modern Karûn (old
Pasitigris) see Dl^Pa 177. 189. 329^; in
upper part perhaps also = modern Kerkhah (= Choaspes), which. was formerly connected with
Karûn not far from Susa (Loftus^Trav. & Researches, 423 ff^
Schaff-Herzog^iii. 2178,art.Shushan^).
