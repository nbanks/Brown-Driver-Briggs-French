Biblical Hebrew

נָשֶׁה
noun [masculine]
a vein (or nerve) in the thigh (etymology dubious; on formative compare Lag^BN 50^; Arabic
[placeholder3465: Placeholders/3465.gif] apparently the portion of the principal vein of the leg which is in the thigh
, vulgar sciatic vein, or (modern) sciatic nerve and even
sciatica, Lane^3033^); — only
גִּיד הַנָּשֶׁה Gen Gen 32:33a the sinew of the thigh-vein (or
thigh-nerve, אֲשֶׁר עַלכַּֿף הַיָּרֵךְ), compare v33b. — On sacredness of thigh,
compare RS^Sem i. 360; 2nd ed. 380^
We^Heid^ (2), 168.
