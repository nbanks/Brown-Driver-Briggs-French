Biblical Hebrew

חֲנָנִי
proper name, masculine (perhaps abbreviated from following); —

## SPLIT 1 sense

1. father of the prophet Jehu 1Kgs 16:1; 1Kgs 16:7; 2Chr 16:7; 2Chr 19:2; 2Chr 20:34.

## SPLIT 2 sense

2. brother of Nehemiah Neh 1:2; Neh 7:2.

## SPLIT 3 sense

3. a chief musician of David 1Chr 25:4; 1Chr 25:25.

## SPLIT 4 sense

4. a chief musician in time of Nehemiah Neh 12:36.

## SPLIT 5 sense

5. a priest of Ezra's time Ezra 10:20.
