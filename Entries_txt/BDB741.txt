Biblical Hebrew

[אֲרוּמָה] proper name [of a location]
home of Abimelek (Thes Add below ארם)
Judg 9:41
בָּארוּמָה, probably = רוּמָה
2Kgs 23:36,
compare Jerome
Lag^Onomast. 146, 2nd ed. 178^. It must have lain near Shechem;
identified by MV and others with El `Ormah, 2 hours
southeast from Shechem (van de Velde^Reisen ii. 268^), but
this place not otherwise known; compare Survey^ii. 387^.
