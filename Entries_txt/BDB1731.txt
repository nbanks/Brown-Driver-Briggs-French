גָּרֵב

@@SPLIT:sense@@

1.
proper name, masculine (compare Palmyrene proper name, masculine גריבא
Vog^141^) one of David's heroes 2Sam 23:38 = 1Chr 11:40.

@@SPLIT:sense@@

proper name [of a location] (compare Sabean proper name, of a location גרבם
Hal^No. 379, 382^) only in גִּבְעַת גָּרֵב
Jer 31:39
hill of Gareb, apparently close to Jerusalem on the southwest compare Gf; see also Hi
Che.
