Νεεμιας(‐ου), etc.; — proper name נחמיה(וׅ on Israel scarab in BM, ClGann^Jas 1883, Fév.-Mars. 156 No. 42^); —

1. the son of Hachaliah, governor of Judah under Artaxerxes Longimanus Neh 1:1; Neh 8:9; Neh 10:2; Neh 12:26; Neh 12:47.

2. ruler of half the district of Bethzur Neh 3:16.

3. one of twelve heads of people who returned with Zerubbabel Ezra 2:2 = Neh 7:7.
