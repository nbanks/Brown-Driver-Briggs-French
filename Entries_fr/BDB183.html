BDB183 [H226]

hébreu biblique

אוֺת nom masculin79 Ex 4,8 (féminin Jos 24,17 ) signe (arabe , plural , araméen אָתָא, ) — ׳ א Gn 4,15 +; construct Gn 9,12 +; plural אוֺתוֺת Ex 4,9 + etc. —

1. signe, pledge, token Gn 4,15 ( J ); אוֺת אֱמֶת marque vraie Jos 2,12 ( J ); of blood of passover Ex 12,13 ( P ); אוֺת לְטוֺבָה marque en bien Ps 86,17 gages, assurances of travellers Jb 21,29.
2. signes, omens promised by prophets as pledges of certain predicted events 1 S 10,7; 1 S 10,9 + 1 S 10,1 where ׳ א insert ᵐ5 ᵑ9 , see We Dr; especially phrase זֶה הָאוֺת לְ Ex 3,12; 1 S 2,34; 1 S 14,10; 2 R 19,29 ; Jer 44,29; Isa 7,11; Isa 7,14 probably also Es 44,25 (of false prophets).
3. signe, symbol of prophets Es 8,18 compare Ez 4,3.
4. signes, miracles, as pledges or attestations of divine presence & interposition Ex 4,8 (twice in verse); Ex 4,9 ( J ) Ex 7,3 ( P ) Ex 8,19 ( J ) Ps 74,9 ; 2Kgs 19,29; 2 R 20,8; 2 R 20,9 = Es 37,30; Es 38,7; Es 38,22 with עָשָׂה Ex 4,17; Ex 4,30; Nb 14,11; Nb 14,22; Jos 24,17 (all J E ) Dt 11,3 ; Jg 6,17 with צִוָּה Ex 4,28 ( J ); with שִׁית Ex 10,1 ( J ); with שִׂים Ex 10,2 ( J ) Ps 78,43; Es 66,19 הָאוֺת וְהַמּוֺפֵת (see מוֺפֵת ) Dt 13,3 compare Dt 28,46; Es 20,3 ׳ אוֺ מ ׳ א Dt 13,2 אוֺתוֺת וּמוֺפְתִים Dt 4,34; Dt 7,19; Dt 26,8; Dt 29,2; Jr 32,31 with נָתַן Dt 6,22; Ne 9,10 with שִׂים Jr 32,20; Ps 105,27 with שָׁלַח Dt 34,11; Ps 135,9.
5. signes, memorials, stones from Jordan Jos 4,6 ( J ); metal of censers Nb 17,3 ( P ); Aaron's rod Nb 17,25 ( P ); עוֺלָם ׳ א Es 55,13 probably also Ezek 14,8 (|| מָשָׁל); signes on hands, etc., Ex 13,9; Ex 13,16 ( J ) = Dt 6,8 ; Deut 11,18, probably belong here; also memorial pillar in Egypt Es 19,20.
6. signe, pledge of covenant, הַבְּרִית ׳ א (see ברית) p. ex. rainbow, of Noachian convenant Gn 9,12; Gn 9,13; Gn 9,17 ( P ); circumcision, of Abrahamic covenant Gn 17,11 ( P ); the sabbath Ex 31,13; Ex 31,17 ( P ); Ez 20,12; Ez 20,20.
7. ensignes, standards Nb 2,2 ( P ) Ps 74,4 .
8. signes, tokens of changes of weather & times Gn 1,14 ( P ; of heavenly luminaries) אוֺתוֺת הַשָּׁמַיִם Jr 10,2 (changes of the heavens as omens to frighten the nations) compare Ps 65,9.
