=== BDB2173 H1947 ===
hébreu biblique

[הוֺלֵלָה] nom féminin
madness (on text voir ci-dessous), seulement pluriel הֹלֵלוֺת, הוֺלֵלוֺת,
and seulement Qohéleth:
Qo 1,17
and I set my heart (וָאֶתְּנָה לִבִּי) to know wisdom, and to
know madness and folly (הֹלֵלוֺת וְשִׂכְלוּת), comparer הוֺלֵלוֺת
Qo 2,12 (||
id.); ׳
וְלָדַעַת רֶשַׁע כֶּסֶל וְהַסִּכְלוּת ה
Qo 7,25 c.-à-d. to
know folly to be madness; possibly read חוֺלֵלוּת, comparer סִכְלוּת &
following; the moral evil of it is specifically recognized in Qo 9,3 (|| רָע).
