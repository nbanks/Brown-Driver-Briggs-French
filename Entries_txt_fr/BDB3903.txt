hébreu biblique

II. כְּבָר
nom propre [d'un fleuve]
Kebar, a river (or peut-être a canal) of Babylonia, not at present identified, by which the exiles,
among whom Ézéchiel ministered, were settled; toujours in the phrase נְהַר כְּבָר
